Ok. Here goes......

- Nighttrain by GNR is stuck inside my head. Especially the last 90 seconds and guitar-outro of that song. In his prime, Slash totally owned everyone....

- Don't work hard. Work smart. Follow the Pareto principle. Identify what's really adding to your team's bottomline. Do those well. The rest of the activities are just sideshows. Eliminate them.

- Learn that 'urgent' and 'important' are not the same things. Urgent can wait. Important cannot. Focus on whatever is important.....

- But never take shortcuts. Roll up your sleeves when you have to. Often times, there is absolutely no substitute for hard, grunt work. Observe, learn, absorb, do. Repeat.

- Thanks to Spotify, I've discovered that people have awful taste in music. Hits of Michael Bolton? Seriously....? 😄

- Ah, Seattle summer. All 78 glorious minutes of it.....

- Make a decision. Even a bad decision is better than not making one. I've made several seemingly important decisions in 30 seconds. Some worked. Some didn't. But I've never had the regret of NOT taking a decision when I needed to.

- Alice was lost in wonderland. She came to a cross-road and didn't know which path to take. She saw the Cheshire cat sitting nearby and decided to ask him -

    _Cat: "That depends a good deal on where you want to get to"_

    _Alice: "I don't much care where--"_

    _Cat: "Then it doesn't matter which way you go"_

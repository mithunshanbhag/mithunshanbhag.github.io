Turn your journal into daily comic strips, with AI helping translate your reflections into visual stories.

## Step 1: Upload selfies

![original picture](../../../images/comic-original.png)

## Step 2: Likeness / avatars generated

The app will automatically generate avatars based on your selfies. You can choose from a variety of styles and expressions to represent yourself in the comic strips.

![generated avatars](../../../images/comic-avatar.png)

## Step 3: Get daily personalized comic strips

As you update your daily journal, the app will generate daily comic strips based on your entries.

Each strip will reflect your thoughts, anecdotes, interactions, observations, and experiences in a fun and engaging way.
